Lasagna

Description

This is the recipe for a home-made lasagna that has been in the family for generations.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Pasta Sheets
  • Cheese
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Brown the Ground Beef and add the Garlic
  2. Cook the Pasta Sheets
  3. Put a layer Ground Beef then a layer of Pasta Sheets
  4. Repeat Step three until Ground Beef and Pasta Sheets are finished
  5. Add the cheese on top and put it in the oven to brown
