Chicken Salad

Description

This is the recipe for a delicious chicken salad that has been in the family for generations.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast Fillets
  • Cabbage
  • Feta Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Fry the breast fillets in a pan with the garlic
  2. Cut all the fresh ingredients
  3. Toss all the fresh ingredients into a salad bowl
  4. Cut the breast fillets into bite sized pieces
  5. Add the breast fillet pieces to the salad bowl with the other ingredients
  6. Toss the salad to your liking and serve
